Gowun Dodum(고운돋움) is a humanistic san-serif typeface with a touch of hand movement. Gowun means ‘neat and delicate’ in Korean and it has a warm and friendly impression.


It is designed especially for Hangeul(Korean script) but it includes Latin script as a part of KS X 1001. This typeface has regular weight with 2,350 basic Hangeul syllables at this moment.

There is also sibling family, Gowun Batang(고운바탕)
